The C.O.R.E. Prompt System

Four inputs.
Consistent output. Every time.

Most AI frustration comes from vague inputs. C.O.R.E. gives you a repeatable system so every prompt produces something worth using.

C
Context

Tell the AI who you are and what world you operate in. Church size, denomination, congregation demographics, preaching style. The more specific, the less generic the output.

"I'm a lead pastor at a 400-person evangelical church. My congregation skews 35-55 and values expository preaching."
O
Outcome

Define the specific deliverable. Not "help me with my sermon" — the actual output, length, and format you need.

"Write a 150-word opening illustration for a sermon on Psalm 23 that connects to the feeling of being overwhelmed at work."
R
Rules

Constraints, voice, and what to avoid. This is where your pastoral voice lives. No clichés. Specific length. Tone requirements. The guardrails that make the output usable.

"Warm and direct. No religious clichés. Under 200 words. Don't reference social media."
E
Example

Show it what good looks like. Paste in a paragraph from a sermon that landed well. This single step cuts editing time in half.

"Here's a paragraph from last week's intro that landed well: [paste your own words]"
